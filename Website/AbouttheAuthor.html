Hi, my name is Gunnar Bivens. I'm an amateur paleontologist & paleoartist currently studying at Arizona State University. I specialize in sauropod dinosaurs, but I am broadly interested in dinosaurs & other prehistoric animals as a whole. I also work for FossilCrates, & you can find my skeletal reconstructions used in much of their work.

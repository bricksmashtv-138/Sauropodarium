Genus Coloradisaurus Galton, 1990
=Coloradia Bonaparte, 1978 (preoccupied name)
="Coloradisaurus" Lambert, 1983 (nomen nudum)

Coloradisaurus brevis Bonaparte, 1978
=Coloradia brevis Bonaparte, 1978
="Coloradisaurus" brevis Lambert, 1983
Late Triassic
Los Colorados Formation
Argentina
PVL 3967 (Holotype)
Diagnosis - enter
Comments - The original name Coloradia was preoccupied by a moth (Blake, 1863). Lambert (1983) has been near universally cited to be the establishment of the replacement name Coloradisaurus that has been used since, however he never explicitly erected this as a replacement name, as he had been informed by Bonaparte that he knew of the homonymy & was intending on publishing Coloradisaurus himself, which never occurred. Thus, Lambert's name was in fact a nomen nudum, & it would not be until Galton (1990) that the name Coloradisaurus was formalized, albeit unintentionally (Greenfield, Bivens, & Fonseca, 2020).

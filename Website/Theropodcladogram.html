This phylogeny is the author's opinion based on their interpretation of the literature. It is however, broadly congruent with recent works (e.g. Tschopp, Mateus, & Benson, 2015; McPhee, et al. 2018; Moore, et al. 2023; Gorscak, et al. 2023; Poropat, et al. 2023).

Based on the Theropod Database (which is roughly based on Mike Keesey's old Dinosauricon site). Beta and Gamma are sister taxa within the clade Alpha Delta is within Gamma, but not a member of Zeta. Epsilon, however, is Gamma incertae sedis. Eta is only tentatively placed within Zeta. Nomina nuda are non-italicized and placed in quotation marks. Nomina dubia are not distinguished from valid taxa, as which taxa are indeterminate is a subjective question.
